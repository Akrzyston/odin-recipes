Scrambled Eggs

Description

Unlock the simple yet sublime pleasure of a breakfast classic with our Fluffy Scrambled Eggs recipe. Whisked to perfection, our scrambled eggs are a delightful canvas of creamy, velvety goodness, enriched with just a hint of butter and seasoned with a touch of salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack eggs on a flat surface (helps ensure shells stay out of the mix) into a bowl that's deep enough for some serious whisking.
  2. To make scrambled eggs on the stove, melt a teaspoon of butter or olive oil, or a combination of both, in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Butter and oil add rich flavor to the eggs and help prevent sticking, but you can also use cooking spray.
  3. When the butter starts to bubble, or a drop of water added to the pan sizzles, slowly pour in the egg mixture. Immediately reduce the heat to medium-low and as soon as the eggs begin to "set" or form a slightly solid base (a minute or two), gently stir with a rubber spatula.
  4. As soft curds begin to form, add minced herbs, shredded cheese, crumbled bacon, chopped scallions, saut√©ed mushrooms, chopped tomato, or anything else you like. Gently fold those ingredients into the eggs. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Gently scrap the bottom of the pan, turning the eggs until almost all of the egg mixture has cooked through but there are still a few wet areas. Remove the pan from the heat and continue to stir until the eggs finish cooking using only the residual heat from the pan.
